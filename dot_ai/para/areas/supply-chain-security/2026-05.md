### `@xmldom/xmldom` shipping-to-users status

`@nx/maven@22.7.1` declares `"@xmldom/xmldom": "^0.8.10"`.

- `^0.8.10` resolves to 0.8.13 on fresh installs (latest 0.8.x, the safe one).
- 0.8.13 is **NOT** deprecated. 0.8.10, 0.8.11, 0.8.12 are deprecated with critical-issues message.
- Users with stale lockfiles or pinned to 0.8.10–0.8.12 are vulnerable.
- The `^0.9.0` line was also published recently (latest 0.9.10, 2026-04-18) but `^0.8.10` won't resolve into it.

**Net:** New users today get safe code, but the floor advertised in the manifest is a registry-deprecated version. Bump to `^0.8.13` is a one-line audit-noise reduction with low risk.
