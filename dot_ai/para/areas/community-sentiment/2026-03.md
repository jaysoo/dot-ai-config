## Emerging Patterns

1. **Vite 8 is now a crisis-level gap**: 39 reactions in 4 days, 0 comments. #34849 has broken into the all-time top 10 most-upvoted open issues. @nx/vite is the #1 plugin by downloads (1.6M/week). The mismatch between user demand and response is the single biggest perception risk right now.

2. **Angular-Rspack performance is the new frontier**: #34936 (incremental build perf) filed today with immediate investigation. Rspack is being positioned as Angular's next build tool, but perf parity with Webpack is not yet achieved. This will be a recurring theme as more teams adopt angular-rspack.

3. **Module Federation still in crisis**: #34903 (security vuln, unresponded), #34083 (production broken, 15+ comments), #34809 (Angular 21 MF), #34584 (ESM exports), #34627 (HMR). Discussion asking about MF enhanced v2 migration path. No clear resolution timeline on any of these.

4. **Enterprise observability demand**: Pylon tickets show strong enterprise demand for Prometheus/Datadog integration, CI metrics OLTP endpoints, failure/flakiness metrics. Customers want to pipe NX Cloud data into their existing monitoring stacks. This is a product opportunity.

5. **Self-healing CI traction**: 4 Pylon tickets about self-healing in the last month. Customers asking about time limits, fix statistics, and evaluation. Feature is generating real interest but needs better visibility/docs.

6. **Supply chain security -- one down, one to go**: #34875 (serialize-javascript) resolved. #34903 (@module-federation/enhanced) still open. The copy-webpack-plugin supply chain issue was patched but module-federation has its own dependency chain problems.

7. **Docker/lockfile cluster slowly shrinking**: Down from 10 to ~5 open issues. Some resolved, some fell out of recent sample. Still a persistent area but improving.

8. **nx release independent projects still rough**: #34542 (workspace-level bumps), #34438 (changelog for independent), #34211 (regressions, 6 reactions), #34034 (dependent libs). Users adopting nx release for complex setups are hitting edge cases.

9. **AI/MCP adoption signals**: Both OSS (Codex sandbox discussion, .agents sharing) and enterprise (3 Pylon tickets on MCP/LLM). AI integration transitioning from experiment to expected capability.
