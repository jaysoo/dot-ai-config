## Summary

- **Downloads surging**: 36.4M npm downloads in Feb 2026, up 75% YoY from 20.8M in Feb 2025, and up 10% MoM from 33.2M in Jan 2026. Weekly downloads hit ~9.3M.
- **Active development**: 231 PRs merged in February; 3 stable releases (22.4.5, 22.5.0–22.5.3) plus 22.6.0 beta cycle (beta.0 through beta.7). Team is shipping fast.
- **Bundler/Rspack issues dominate**: 7 of 56 new open issues relate to bundlers (Rspack, Webpack, Vite). The Angular+Rspack integration is particularly rough.
- **Long-standing feature debt persists**: Top-voted open issues (71, 69, 63 thumbs-up) are 3–5 years old — tsconfig generation, type-check command, silent mode. These are not new but continue to accumulate votes.
- **Security posture improved**: The s1ngularity supply chain attack (Aug 2025) is behind us, and the recent minimatch CVE-2026-26996 was patched same-day (PR #34660).
