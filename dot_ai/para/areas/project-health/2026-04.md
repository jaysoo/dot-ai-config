## Proposed Process Rules

1. **Administrative close rule**: After 6+ consecutive audit flags, audit owner may close zombie projects administratively with 48h objection window. Current case: Gradle + Self-Healing CI at 12 flags.
2. **Auto-flag rule** (from prior reports, still not adopted): If all milestones 100% and no new milestones in 60+ days, auto-flag for graduation.
3. **Target date required**: Projects must have target dates before entering "In Progress."
4. **PoV > 3mo → Implementation**: Reclassify Enterprise PoVs over 3 months as "Enterprise Implementation."
5. **Deprecated team → auto-cancel**: When a team is deprecated, its projects auto-cancel unless reassigned within 30 days.
