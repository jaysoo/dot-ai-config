Three advisories (one high, two moderate but aggregated as high): GHSA-v2wj-q39q-566r, GHSA-p9ff-h696-f583, GHSA-4w7w-66w2-5vf9. Dev-server only but still worth patching.

4. **@tootallnate/once chain (low, angular + react)** — Via `jsdom`. Upstream jsdom upgrade path is breaking. Low risk.
